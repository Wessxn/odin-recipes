Summer Pasta with Basil, Tomatoes and Cheese

Ingredients

  1. 2 pounds vine ripened tomatoes, seeded and diced
  2. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  3. ½ cup chopped fresh basil
  4. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves
  5. ¾ teaspoon salt
  6. ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  7. ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
